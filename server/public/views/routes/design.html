Adventures in Design & Marketing

I began my career as a professional graphic designer and art director, working for non-profits, small agencies and freelancing. I greatly enjoyed developing logos and corporate identity, which led me to pursue marketing.

After getting an MBA in marketing, I worked in product management and communications for companies and non-profits at which I was able to continue to use my design background. In my most recent position, as head of the marketing team at a small medical supplies distributor, I discovered my fascination with the technology used by marketing. This led me to pursue a career in software development, focusing on front end where I can use my design and marketing skills and experience to enrich my software development work.
