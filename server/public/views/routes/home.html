Hospital Disinfectant Selector

This solo project allows hospital personnel to get information on a disinfectant product quickly to facilitate prevention and control of outbreak situations. A healthcare worker fills out the very short questionnaire, and receives product information about the best products for their scenario.

Hotel Room Assigner for Boating Team

A group project for the administrator of a boat racing team, this application allows the admin to set up information and send surveys to her team about their race attendance and hotel preferences. The team chooses their hotel room, with full editing allowed by the admin. You may login using the username visitor and password 123 .
