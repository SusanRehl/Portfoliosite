Hospital Disinfectant Selector

This application allows hospital and long-term care facility personnel to get technical and regulatory information about hospital-grade disinfectant products quickly, which helps to prevent and control outbreak situations. A healthcare worker fills out the short questionnaire describing their usage scenario, and receives information about the highest ranked products for their scenario.

Check out the Selector Checkout code

Hotel Room Assigner for Boating Team

A group project for the administrator of a boat racing team, this application allows the admin to set up information and send surveys to the team about their race attendance and hotel room preferences. Then each team member chooses their hotel room, with full editing allowed by the admin. Reports are also generated to send to the hotel for final reservations.
