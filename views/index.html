Adventures in Coding

I'm a software engineer who is always adventuring into new projects and new technologies. Click on the images to check out my hosted projects.

Hospital Disinfectant Selector

This solo project allows hospital personnel to get information on a disinfectant product quickly to facilitate prevention and control of outbreak situations. The healthcare worker fills out the very short questionnaire, and receives product information on 3 of the best products for their scenario.

Hotel Room Assigner for Boating Team

A group project for the administrator of a boat racing team, this application allows the admin to set up information and send surveys to her team about their race attendance and hotel preferences. The team chooses their hotel room, with full editing allowed by the admin.
